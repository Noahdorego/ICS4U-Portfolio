ABOUT ME

I am Noah do Rego, a student in my final year of highschool at A.Y. Jackson. I've been interested in coding and computer science ever since I was 10 years old. I started with block-based coding on Scratch, but have expanded my knowledge by trying many different languages, including C++, C#, JS, HTML, CSS, Python, and more. I am interested in game development, UI/UX design, web development and mobile development.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading, playing sports, working on personal projects and playing video games. Recently, I've been hooked on a game called Rocket League, an easy to pick up yet hard to master car-soccer hybrid.

MODULES

Throughout the course, I've worked on many projects. A large part of my learning was focused on Khan Academy, which is an intuitive, immersive platform. However, I also experimented with other platforms- I worked with Javascript on Codecademy, and began the machine learning certification on FreeCodeCamp, but could not complete a project due to time constraints. Another resource that I used is the p5.js web editor, which helped me display my projects outside of Khan Academy and served as a tool in the coding challenge presentation. I plan on continuing to use these resources in my spare time to develop my coding skills and create projects of varying complexity to add to my personal portfolio.
